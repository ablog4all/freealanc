01A Collection of Poems

Swimming

Step by step

slowly

I resist

Until

Letting go

I am floating.

Avg. distance

384,400 km

Est. travel time

3 days

Journey

We set out

From the unknown.

Along the way,

declaring this as ourselves

And that, our home.

Crying out:

"This is me, here I am"

Eventually realizing,

The destination

Is itself the unknown

Avg. distance

225 mil. km

Est. travel time

9 months

wheel

Wanting to go forward,

I wind up spinning

Around,

Over and over;

Although I am spiralling

I am still moving

Straight toward

My destination.

Avg. distance

628 mil. km

Est. travel time

3 years

Resolution

A peaceful ending,

A reach toward dreams,

A dedication,

A search for the answer.

Resolve,

If you are going to,

toward something

too good to be true.

Avg. distance

1.6 bil. km

Est. travel time

7 years
